# Finals Buddy — Visual Identity

tone: calm, encouraging, clear-headed
imagery: warm and studious; soft daylight, paper and desk textures, generous negative space; real students over stock clichés

## Personality
Warm. Focused. Reassuring. A study companion that lowers exam stress instead of adding to it — cozy but organized, never clinical.

## Do
- Keep layouts uncluttered; let the logo breathe (clear space = logo height).
- Use the palette in `color-system.json` exactly — sage, warm-brown, cream, dusty-rose. No eyeballed colors.
- Pair Fraunces (display serif) headlines with Outfit (sans) body for the signature warm-scholarly voice.

## Don't
- Stretch, rotate, or recolor the logo.
- Set body text below the `minContrast` ratio in `color-system.json`.
- Reach for cool grays or neon — the neutral ramp is warm.

## Reference notes
Palette and fonts are taken directly from the shipped product UI: sage/cream/warm-brown surfaces with a dusty-rose accent, Fraunces display + Outfit body. Live demo: https://finals-buddy.vercel.app
